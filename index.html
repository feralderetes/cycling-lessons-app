Free cycling lessons

All ages welcome!

Learning how to ride a bike can be challenging at any age. But let's face it, the older we get the greater the fear of falling and hurting ourselves. If you are wanting to experience the pleasure and freedom that a bike can provide but are wondering if you can still learn at this age... The answer is: "Absolutely you can!".
From 0 to 100+ years old you can join our classes, where we will teach you everything you need to know about riding techniques, safety, basics of maintanance and repairs, general traffic rules and different tips and tricks to make sure you enjoy your ride while keeping yourself (and others) safe.
We offer group lessons for children, teenagers and adults absolutely free of charge! All you need to bring is your bike, a helmet and a highly motivated spirit.
